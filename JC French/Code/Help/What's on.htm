What's on the LinguaMaster CD?

LinguaMaster gives you the complete official Department of Education recordings and transcripts, and more.

There's hundreds of phrases and items of vocabulary to learn. As well as helping you in the Oral and Aural exams, the 'on click' dictionary will build your knowledge of expressions for the Written Exams: be it writing an essay or to support an argument.

The words and phrases have all come up in the exams before, and you can be sure that a lot of them will come up again.
