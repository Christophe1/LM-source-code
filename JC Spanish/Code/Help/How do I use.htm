How do I use LinguaMaster?

In LinguaMaster, when you pick 1 of the years on the left of the screen, a play bar and tapescript appear.

The play bar is for controlling the sound: the official Department of Education Listening Comprehension recording that came up in the year being studied.

The tapescript provides a text of the recording, with hundreds of words, phrases and expressions to click on. When clicked, they provide you with a translation.

On the tapescript, pick a part of a section. Read through the tapescript as you listen to the recording. Listen to it all the way through, first of all, to get an overview of the topic being discussed.
