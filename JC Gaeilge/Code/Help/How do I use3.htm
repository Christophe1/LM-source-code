How do I use Lingua-Master?

Make an effort to incorporate the new words and phrases that you learn with LinguaMaster into your school activities: essays or role plays. Try to organise with a person or a group of people to have conversations in the language being studied. Talk about everyday situations: your family life, hobbies and pass-times. All phrases and vocabulary in LinguaMaster are 100% relevant, as they have all come up in the exams before.

LinguaMaster is an invaluable tool, not only for the oral and aural, but for all areas of language learning. If you use LinguaMaster diligently throughout the year you can be guaranteed to achieve your full potential in the Aural exam.
