How do I use LinguaMaster?

If you don't understand a highlighted word or phrase, click on it for a translation. Take notes of new words and phrases that you hear. Print out the dictionary to view and learn off the many phrases and words that arise year in year out.

Use the play bar to replay phrases and expressions, and fine tune your hearing for the Oral and Aural exams.

This structure - listening to the content all the way through and then in stages - is the way the Aural exam will be presented to you in the exam. It is good practice to use LinguaMaster in this way: first get an overall idea of the content, and then pay attention to it in more detail.

Use LinguaMaster in conjunction with your past aural exam papers to prepare you for the Listening Comprehension part of the exam.

LinguaMaster is an invaluable tool, not only for the oral and aural, but for all areas of language learning. If you use LinguaMaster diligently throughout the year you can be guaranteed to achieve your full potential in the Aural exam.
